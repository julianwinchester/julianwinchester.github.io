SUN

The sun is a medium sized star.Its Gravity holds the eleven planets.Its the closest star to Earth.It's the energy source that makes life possible on Earth.The surface of the Sun averages about 10,000 degrees f.The Sun is made of 4 layers:The core,The Radiative zone,The Convection zone,and the Photosphere.The Sun is about 93,000,000 miles away from Earth.The Sun makes energy from a process called Thermonuclear fusion.Many Ancient cultures worshipped the Sun.
